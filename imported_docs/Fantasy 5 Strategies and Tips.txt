Use the Following Pattern to Play California Fantasy 5
Tips 1. Choose Numbers From Different Groups
California Fantasy 5 has 39 numbers, Number sets can be mixed freely according to your needs. For example, we can split these numbers into 4 groups 1-10, 11-20, 21-30, 31-39.

We analyzed all California Fantasy 5 historical data, most of them spread across different groups. However, sometimes, one or more of the number groups is missing. The more groups that are split, the more groups that are missed.

Tips 2. Choose Low Number and High Number Ratio
Split the 39 numbers into two groups. The low half is 01 to 19, and the high half is 20 to 39. We have analyzed many lottery draw results, most winning numbers are made up of low field and high field. In rare cases, the winning numbers are all low field numbers or all high field numbers.

Basically, there are only 1-4 winning numbers in a low group or high group. The winning numbers most occurrence ratio of low to high are 3:2, 2:3, 1: 4, 4: 1.

Tips 3. Choose Odd Number and Even Number Ratio
We have analyzed many lottery draw results, most winning numbers are made up of odd numbers and even numbers. In rare cases, the winning numbers are all odd numbers or all even numbers.

Split the numbers into two groups. odd numbers in a group, and even numbers in another group. Basically, there are only 1-4 winning numbers in an odd group or even group. Counting all the all odd/even groups, there are no combinations where all 5 numbers are even and only 1 combination where all 5 numbers are odd. The winning numbers most occurrence ratio of odd to even are 3:2, 2:3, 1: 4, 4: 1.

Tips 4. Choose Adjacent Numbers of Previous Winning Numbers
What is the adjacent number? If number 21 has been hit in the last drawn, its adjacent numbers(19, 20, 22, 23) are likely to be drawn.

Analyzed all California Fantasy 5 historical data. We found a pattern by looking at the drawings trend chart, a number that appeared in the previous drawn has a high probability of appearing in its adjacent numbers.

Tips 5. Use Numbers Sum Value to Exclude Some Combinations
We analyzed all California Fantasy 5 historical data and came to the conclusion that the sums of the winning numbers are basically in a range. The sum range was basically 50-140. So before betting on numbers, exclude combinations where the sum is too low or too high.

Tips 6. Use Numbers Average Value to Exclude Some Combinations
We analyzed all California Fantasy 5 historical data and came to the conclusion that the Average Value of the winning numbers are basically in a range. The Average Value range was basically 10-25. So before betting on numbers, exclude combinations where the sum is too low or too high.

Tips 7. Use AC Value to Exclude Some Combinations
We analyzed all California Fantasy 5 historical data, The most often occurring AC Value are 5 and 6, over 90%.
